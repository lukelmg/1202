Engineered Travel Cases

Frequent travelers know that packing toiletries can be challenging. At Highv, we've developed a unique case that's big enough for your daily products, but small enough to fit in a personal carry on item. Our patent-pending design includes designated spaces for each item that snap in and out of place. The molded plastic case utilizes moisture lock hinges to prevent unwanted spills while protecting the contents of your bag. And for those questionable surfaces you might encounter along your journey, a hidden stand can be deployed to keep your case and your products elevated.

Highv reimagines the world or toiletry cases, designed to streamline your lifestyle on the go.

The Inspiration

We're a group of students pursuing engineering degrees at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. For our freshman final design project we created a toiletry case, inspired by the honeycombs in man-made honey bee nests known as hives. We wanted to create a space perfectly designed for its contents and nothing more, suitable for use on a camping trip, flying high in the air, and everything in between. Our goal is to improve upon aspects of daily life through innovative design, and Highv is just the beginning.
